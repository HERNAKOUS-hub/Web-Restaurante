La Tintorería

Restaurant in the heart of Madrid

C/ Espoz y Mina, 20. 28012 Madrid

Traditional menu with imaginative touches
Kitchen always open

Toasts, raciones (tapas), and Mediterranean recipes in the brick and exposed stone cellars of a 17th-century building. A historic setting that creates an intimate and charming atmosphere.

The name La Tintorería (The Dry Cleaner's) is no coincidence: it's a nod to our carefully selected range of wines. Reds, whites, rosés, and sparkling wines designed for every taste and budget. A cellar conceived to accompany every dish, every moment, and every type of diner.

At La Tintorería, a brutal gastronomic experience awaits you: outrageously good food, made with care and creativity, for a price that won't empty your wallet.

Ideal for those who value authentic flavour, thoughtful service, and the pleasure of eating well without complications.
